Gargi Beauty

Gargi Beauty, headquartered in Mumbai, a brand with the 15 years of experience which aims at formulating lip balms that not only prevent the lips from drying and chapping, but also moisturize them. Ayurveda, India’s traditional system of medicine dating its legacy back to more than 5000 years, serves as a basis for these lip balms that are rich in precious herbal extracts. The blend of the Indian herbal extracts with handpicked ingredients is the ideal daily regime to soothe and hydrate your lips. With the use of best and advanced technology, we aim to be a major player in every product category that we venture and to reach to the new heights by providing polished products. We look forward to serve you with our fine quality.
